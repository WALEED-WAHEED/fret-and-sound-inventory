About Fret & Sound Guitar Shop

Main Screen About

Fret & Sound Guitar Shop is a full-service guitar and bass retailer serving musicians of all levels. We specialize in acoustic guitars, electric guitars, basses, and beginner packs.

Our inventory includes name-brand instruments and parts from trusted manufacturers. We carry in-house assembled components such as machine head tuners and bridges, as well as outsourced parts from companies like Seymour Duncan and Warmoth.

Whether you are buying your first guitar or building a custom instrument, our team is here to help. Visit our main screen to manage inventory and place orders.
